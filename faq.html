FAQ

How does the free trial work?

You get full access for the trial period; charges start after trial ends.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel anytime from account settings. Subscription will remain active until the current paid period ends.

Will I be charged automatically each month?

Yes — subscriptions are recurring until you cancel. (Stripe handles recurring payments.)

What happens if a payment fails?

We’ll notify you to update payment details. After a grace period your subscription may be paused.

Can I share an account with my team?

Yes — Advanced & Pro plans support multiple users/groups as listed in plan details.

Which apps can I connect?

We support many popular services (TikTok, Gmail, Drive, YouTube, Slack, Telegram). We’re adding more integrations constantly.
